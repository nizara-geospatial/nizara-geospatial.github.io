How the System Works

Fully autonomous from satellite to your inbox. No manual steps, no delays.

01 →

Satellite Data Acquisition

Multi-sensor imagery is automatically retrieved from space agency archives every 24 hours via Google Earth Engine API.

Sentinel-2 MSI Sentinel-1 SAR MODIS Terra Landsat 8/9
02 →

Atmospheric Correction & Cloud Masking

Raw imagery is processed: cloud pixels removed via QA bands, surface reflectance normalized for accurate spectral analysis.

QA60 Masking Level-2A SR Median Composite
03 →

Multi-Index Spectral Analysis

Proprietary algorithms compute BSI, NDVI, NDWI, LST and apply multi-criteria decision rules to detect target features with high accuracy.

BSI Algorithm NDVI / NDWI LST Thermal
04 →

Change Detection & Risk Scoring

Bi-temporal comparison identifies new developments vs. previous baseline. Confidence score computed from multi-indicator validation.

30-Day Baseline Kappa Accuracy Wind Physics
05 →

Automated Report Generation & Delivery

7 satellite maps, PDF intelligence report, CSV data, and GeoJSON files are compiled and delivered to client channels automatically.

PDF Report GeoJSON Telegram Email
